Questions
The obvious ones,
answered.
How does the AI actually analyze my site? +
We take a full screenshot of your URL and extract the text content. Both are sent to our AI critic, which evaluates your site across four dimensions using criteria built from hundreds of high-converting product pages. The AI looks at what a real, skeptical visitor would see — not just aesthetics.
Does it work on single-page apps and React sites? +
Yes. We use a headless browser to fully render JavaScript-heavy sites before capturing content. SPAs, React, Vue, Next.js — all supported.
How many free scans do I get? +
3 free scans per day, no signup required. If you need more, Pro gives you unlimited scans, deeper analysis, PDF exports, and rescan history so you can track improvement over time.
How is this different from Google Lighthouse or PageSpeed? +
Lighthouse measures technical performance — load speed, accessibility scores, Core Web Vitals. HypeKick measures persuasion — whether your site actually makes a visitor want to sign up or buy. Totally different job.
Is my site data stored or shared? +
We capture a screenshot and content snapshot to generate your report. We don't store or share your site's data beyond what's needed to produce the analysis. Reports are private to you unless you choose to share a link.
